Our Story

More Than a Cafe,
It's a Feeling

Fox Stories Cafe was born from a love for bold flavours and warm spaces — a place in the heart of Madhapur where every table has a story, and every dish is crafted with genuine care.

Specialising in Chinese, Thai, and Asian fusion cuisine, our extensive menu spans starters, momos, noodles, fried rice, sizzlers, and fresh sushi. Every visit is a new chapter.

— The Fox Stories Family
